Calotropis

The Sacred Crown Flower

Beauty Significance

  • Calotropis flowers are known for their unique star-shaped beauty.
  • Commonly used in temple decorations and religious ceremonies.
  • Symbolizes strength, purity, and devotion.
  • Its soft lavender and white shades add elegance to floral arrangements.

Herbal Benefits

  • Used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.
  • Latex and leaves are applied (carefully) for skin-related issues.
  • Helps in relieving joint pain when used in herbal preparations.
  • Traditionally used for digestive and respiratory problems.
  • Has antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties whwn used as a ointments.

Important Note

Calotropis contains milky latex which can be toxic if misused. It should only be used under proper guidance from herbal experts.

About Amaltas

Amaltas, also known as Cassia Fistula or the Golden Shower Tree, is famous for its bright yellow flowers. It is widely found in India and is known for its ornamental beauty and medicinal properties.

Beauty Benefits

  • Enhances garden beauty with bright yellow hanging flowers.
  • Used in landscaping for decorative purposes.
  • Natural shade provider during summer.
  • Symbol of prosperity and positivity in many cultures.
  • Used in floral decorations and festivals.

Herbal Benefits

  • Used in Ayurveda for treating constipation.
  • Helps in detoxifying the body.
  • Beneficial for skin infections and inflammation.
  • Used in herbal remedies for fever and cough.
  • Improves digestion and supports liver health.

Chenopodium (Bathua)

Beauty & Herbal Benefits

About Chenopodium

Chenopodium, commonly known as Bathua, is a nutritious leafy plant widely used in herbal remedies and traditional cooking. It is rich in vitamins A, C, calcium, iron, and antioxidants.

Beauty Benefits

  • Leaf powder of chinopodium improves skin glow due to high Vitamin A content.
  • Gel made up of chinopodium helps to reduce acne and skin infections.
  • Chinopodium powder acts as a natural detox for clearer skin.
  • Conditioner promotes healthy and strong hair growth.
  • Prevents premature aging with antioxidants.

Herbal Benefits

  • Supports healthy digestion.
  • Chinopodium powder helps in treating constipation naturally.
  • Boosts immunity.
  • Rich in iron – helps prevent anemia.
  • Strengthens bones due to high calcium content.

How to Use

Chenopodium leaves can be used in soups, parathas, herbal juices, and traditional remedies. Always wash thoroughly before use.

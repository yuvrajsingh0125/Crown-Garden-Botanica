The poppy flower is known for its bright red, orange, pink, and white petals. It symbolizes peace, remembrance, and beauty. Its delicate petals and dark center make it one of the most eye-catching flowers in gardens and floral arrangements.

  • Used in decorative bouquets
  • Symbol of remembrance and peace
  • Adds vibrant color to gardens
  • Popular in landscape design
